First National Trust - Institutional Overview

First National Trust, established in 1924, stands as one of the premier commercial banking institutions on the East Coast with headquarters in the heart of New York City's financial district. With total assets exceeding $245.7 billion, the bank has weathered numerous economic cycles and continues to serve as a pillar of financial stability in the region.

The institution operates under the routing number 021000021 and SWIFT code FNTRUS33, facilitating both domestic and international transactions for its diverse client base. First National Trust specializes in commercial banking services, offering comprehensive solutions for individual consumers, small businesses, and large corporations alike.

Throughout its nearly century-long history, First National Trust has maintained a commitment to personalized service while embracing technological innovation. The bank was an early adopter of online banking platforms in the 1990s and has since developed a robust mobile banking application that serves over 2 million active users monthly.

First National Trust's investment philosophy emphasizes conservative growth strategies with a focus on capital preservation. The bank's wealth management division oversees approximately $87 billion in client assets, providing portfolio management, estate planning, and retirement planning services. Their team of certified financial planners works closely with clients to develop customized investment strategies aligned with individual risk tolerance and financial goals.

The bank maintains 127 branch locations across New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Pennsylvania, employing over 8,500 professionals. First National Trust has received numerous industry awards, including "Best Commercial Bank in the Northeast" for three consecutive years from Banking Excellence Magazine. The institution is also recognized for its commitment to community development, having invested over $150 million in community development projects and affordable housing initiatives since 2015.

First National Trust operates with a strong capital ratio of 13.2%, well above regulatory requirements, demonstrating financial soundness and the ability to weather economic uncertainties. The bank's loan portfolio is well-diversified across commercial real estate, consumer loans, and business lending, with a non-performing loan ratio of just 0.8%, significantly below the industry average.
